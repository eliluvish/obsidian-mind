# Plan to Extract minr from lab_archives

## Context

[[Kele Piper]] has signaled willingness for the Minor Intake Form to move out of the [[People|people]] app (lab_archives). Eli intends to do this after the current busy season for minr.

This follows the same pattern as [[BioLift|biolift]] and the proposed [[Research Intake Survey|ris]] — standalone apps rather than modules in lab_archives.

## Status

Approved in principle. Waiting for minr busy season to end.
