# Destruction Workflow and DEA Form 41 Process

Source: email from [[Mirabella Daguerre]] (2026-04-05).

## EHS/Triumvirate Involvement

- **No** view-access portal for EHS/Triumvirate in iLog
- They'll receive a **registration-based email** listing the containers a lab wants to destroy

### Current Process

1. AU/Registrant requests disposal
2. Triumvirate schedules disposal with AU/R
3. EHS signs the logbook
4. Triumvirate signs the DEA 41
5. AU/R keeps copy of DEA 41 in a binder

### Proposed Process

1. AU/Registrant requests disposal
2. Triumvirate schedules disposal with AU/R
3. Triumvirate signs DEA 41
4. AU/Registrant **uploads form to iLog**
5. Container status changes from "awaiting destruction" to "destroyed"
6. AU/R signs **per disposal request**, not per container

## Destruction Record Numbering

Each destruction request needs an assigned **record number** to tie the DEA 41 form to containers. E.g., Destruction Record 102 includes container IDs KXY1, KXY2, KXY3. The associated DEA 41 should be visible in each container's lifecycle view.

## DEA Form Upload Concern

> [!warning] Confidentiality Constraint
> DEA forms are confidential documents that **cannot leave the lab**. Labs should not have photos of DEA forms saved on their phones.

Need a camera-capture-only upload flow (similar to bank e-deposits) — opens the camera, captures the image, uploads it without saving to the device's photo library.
