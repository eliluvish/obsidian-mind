# Authorized User Log Rules and Training Changes

Source: email from [[Mirabella Daguerre]] (2026-04-05).

## Authorized User Permissions (confirmed with [[Kele Piper]])

- Authorized Users (AU) **can** add other Authorized Users
- **Registrant** is required to complete the attestation and verify the dates
- Newly added AU does **not** have access to that Registrant's e-logbook until attestation is complete
- Authorized Users **cannot** change the controlled substance registration number

## Training Validation

- **Remove** the training validation column from the Authorized User Log
- Other hospitals are moving away from OpenCourses — controlled substances training is moving to **Healthstream**
- Healthstream does **not** have an accessible API
- This ends the auto-enrollment aspect of the system
- **Replacement**: on-screen reminder that training is required, with a link to the Healthstream course
