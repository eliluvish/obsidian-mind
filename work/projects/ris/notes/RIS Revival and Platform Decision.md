# RIS Revival and Platform Decision

## Timeline

- **2026-03-27**: Eli emailed [[Mirabella Daguerre]] proposing to remove RIS from the People app. Code is ~4 years old and maintaining it in lab_archives is slowing down other work.
- **2026-04-03**: Mirabella confirmed RIS **will be revived** after the [[iLog]] build reaches a less intensive point. Asked how it would function differently on a new platform.
- **2026-04-05**: Eli responded — analogizing to [[BioLift|biolift]] (could live in People but works better standalone). Proposed RIS as its own app. Offered opportunity for a better UI given increased capabilities.
- **2026-04-07**: [[Mirabella Daguerre]] confirmed (after consulting [[Kele Piper]]) — RIS will be extracted from the People app.

## Key Points

- RIS should be a **standalone app**, not part of lab_archives — same direction as [[BioLift|biolift]]
- [[Kele Piper]] has signaled willingness for [[Minor Intake Form|minr]] to also move out of People after its busy season
- Open question from Eli to Mirabella: will RIS still connect to OpenCourses, or does the move to Healthstream (no API) change that? See [[Authorized User Log Rules and Training Changes]]

## Decision (2026-04-07)

[[Mirabella Daguerre]] confirmed (after speaking with [[Kele Piper]]) that RIS will move out of the People app. Current plan is a **standalone app**, but Eli is considering a multi-app solution that could consolidate several smaller apps.

## Status

Standalone app confirmed. Blocked on iLog build intensity — will revive when iLog calms down.
