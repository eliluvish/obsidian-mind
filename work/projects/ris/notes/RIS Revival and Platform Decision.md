# RIS Revival and Platform Decision

## Timeline

- **2026-03-27**: Eli emailed [[Mirabella Daguerre]] proposing to remove RIS from the People app. Code is ~4 years old and maintaining it in lab_archives is slowing down other work.
- **2026-04-03**: Mirabella confirmed RIS **will be revived** after the [[iLog]] build reaches a less intensive point. Asked how it would function differently on a new platform.

- RIS should be a **standalone app**, not part of lab_archives — same direction as [[BioLift|biolift]]

Blocked on iLog build intensity. Will revive when iLog calms down.
